Customer Engagement Director
at Rarely Impossible


Claire heads up Customer Engagement at Rarely Impossible  getting under the skin of client’s business to really understand the problem they are trying to solve. Making sure all along the journey the focus is in the right place, following the Lean Canvas approach.

Claire has been in the industry since 2008 starting as a Junior Web Developer, after having that lucky break. The key factor was finding the right company willing to take a chance on her. Claire has worked her way up through the industry.

Starting as a Web developer has only enhanced her skills she uses today by giving her technical knowledge to bridge the gap between the techies and clients. She is also an active member of the local digital community by attending lots of local meet-up/initiatives.

Wednesday 18th April 2018 4-5pm

What does a career in UX look like? with Damian Rees


"In this talk I'll explore what it's like working as a UXer at Experience UX. I'll use a real project as a case study and take you through each step of the project: the challenges, the research, the decisions, the outcomes.

Then I'll finish with a short overview of some of the things I wish someone had told me when I was at Uni."


Damian is Founder/Director at Experience UX and his fascination with what makes people behave the way they do when using technology drives his passion for user experience. With a degree in Applied Psychology, followed by 18 years’ experience in UX working for companies like the BBC and National Air Traffic Services means that he is an expert in user research and user centred design.

With two kids under two he spends his rare free time catching up on lost sleep, immersed in his Xbox, or sat in a coffee shop somewhere with headphones on trying to write a that novel he can’t finish.

Twitter: @damianrees
experienceux.co.uk

Venue: Itchen Lecture Theatre, Sir James Matthew Building
